  filter(
    trial_type == "catch",

Data collection for Experiment 4 proceeded in two phases.

Eligibility criteria include:

  1. Between 18 and 55 years old;
  2. Having an approval rate of at least 85%;
  3. Speaking English fluently;
  4. No color blindness;
  5. Not having participated in the previous experiments in this project.

We pre-registered three exclusion criteria (see pre-registration and amendment):

  1. Participants who re-started the experiment during the experimental blocks (1 participant);
  2. Those who had more than 10% trials missing from the experimental blocks (2 participant);
  3. Those who chose the HP option on < 9 HP-optimal catch trials (7 participants)

In total, 9 participants were excluded (some met two more more criteria simultaneously), leaving 181 participants.

Data collection for Experiment 4 proceeded in two phases.

Eligibility criteria include:

1.  Between 18 and 55 years old;
2.  Having an approval rate of at least 85%;
3.  Speaking English fluently;
4.  No color blindness;
5.  Not having participated in the previous experiments in this project.

We pre-registered three exclusion criteria (see [pre-registration](https://osf.io/bzv8y) and [amendment](https://osf.io/63rd9)):
